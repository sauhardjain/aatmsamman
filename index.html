The Team The Mission

a social initiative




Aiming emancipation of as many people as possible from impoverished lifestyle, we organise little camps providing basic relief materials to the abundant underprivileged people to help them sustain life. We provide them with such materials at subsidised rates to instil in them a sense of self esteem (AatmSamman) since they are not getting it for free.

  • Winter Drive (Nov'18)

    Relief offered at the onset of Winter 2018. Mobile drives held across rural areas near Civil Lines.

  • Summer Drive (July'19)

    Drives held across Naharpur Slums in Rohini, New Delhi.

  • Fall Drive (Oct'19)

    Largest distribution effor undertaken by us. Items were distributed across the Kashmere Gate area.

Started by 2 high-schoolers in a bid to give back to their community.

an open source


It shall serve as a means for individuals, governments and other business and non-governmental organisations to help address the grievances of those who are often ignored, left uncared for and need a little help to spring from their desolate conditions to decent lifestyles.

the mission


We intend to save the humanity from the stigma associated with poverty created by humanity itself, prevalent across the globe, nucleated in India. This is social initiative initiated to help as many individuals as possible to enjoy the sense of self esteem and further protect their identity in the society.

surplus to deficit


Learning basic economic concepts in high school years, AatmSamman is the practical application of it. We gather all kinds of basic amenities (used/extra) including clothes, food etc. from those who don’t need them anymore and provide them to those whose lives can be saved from the same.

Dignifying the act of receiving, while creating a meaningful model of charity.
