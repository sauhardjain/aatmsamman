a social initiative





Aiming emancipation of as many people as possible from impoverished lifestyle, we organise little camps providing basic relief materials to the abundant underprivileged people to help them sustain life. We provide them with such materials at a subsidised rates to instil in them a sense of self esteem (AatmSamman) since they are not getting it for free.

the mission




We intend to save the humanity from the stigma associated with poverty created by humanity itself, prevalent across the globe, nucleated in India. This is social initiative initiated to help as many individuals as possible to enjoy the sense of self esteem and further protect their identity in the society.
